About my College Education.

I'm glad that I didn't regret that I took up BS Information Technology. My hobby of doing things
in the computer has been a great way for me to introduce me to the technology world. My own
perception about BSIT is that it's a combination of Programming, Multimedia, Networking, and Business.
Just in case, if you're confused with the difference of the aforementioned course with BS
Computer Science is that BSCS deals with Programming, Data Analysis, and Logic. The course is
focused on those fields whereas BSIT is more on the application of technology in the industry.
